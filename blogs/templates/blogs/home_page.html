About Run Records

Run Records, founded by Sebastian Perez (Bastien Groove) in 2015, has earned a reputation in the last decade as an underground dance music label par excellence. It has established itself as a printing option for DJs who value functional, timeless and deep dance music. Run Records go relentlessly to the rhythm of quality, either to unlock talents not discovered in the international market or to launch EP of expert professionals.
